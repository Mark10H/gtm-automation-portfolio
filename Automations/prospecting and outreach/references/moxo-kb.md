## 1A. The HAIFLOW Model — What It Is and How to Use It

**HAIFLOW** stands for **Human + AI Flow**. It is Moxo's core positioning model describing how AI and humans operate together inside every process — AI handles the coordination, execution, and preparation; humans stay accountable for every judgment call and critical decision.

This is not automation that replaces people. It is orchestration that elevates them.

**The one-sentence definition:**
"HAIFLOW is how Moxo works: AI handles the execution and coordination around every step, humans stay accountable for every decision that matters."

**The three-layer model:**

| Layer | Who Does It | What Happens |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Design** | AI + Human | Rep describes the process in plain English. Moxo's AI builds the full workflow — step types, branching logic, roles, routing — automatically. Human reviews and launches. |
| **Orchestrate** | AI Agents | Custom AI agents (built in Agent Studio) run inside the workflow: they pre-fill forms from prior step data, surface recommendations before the human acts, validate submissions after the human completes them, and execute automations between steps. |
| **Run** | Human + AI | Humans complete their assigned steps — approvals, decisions, signatures — with AI assistance at every moment. Every action is logged. Pulse Score tracks health. AI identifies bottlenecks in real time. |

**How to use HAIFLOW in emails and positioning:**
- Lead with the human accountability angle, not the AI replacement angle: "Your team stays in control of every decision — AI handles everything around them."
- Use it to counter full-automation objections: "HAIFLOW is built for processes where human judgment matters. AI handles the coordination overhead so your best people focus on the calls only they can make."
- Use it for AI-forward prospects (Category B triggers): "You're already thinking about AI. HAIFLOW is the model that makes those initiatives operational — AI doesn't just assist, it runs the workflow around every human step."

**Approved HAIFLOW language for emails:**
- "Humans stay accountable, AI handles the coordination"
- "AI handles execution, humans own every decision"
- "The HAIFLOW model keeps your team in control while AI eliminates the overhead"
- "Describe your process in plain English, AI builds it, you're running the same day"

**Never say:**
- "Moxo replaces your team with AI"
- "Fully automated" (HAIFLOW is always human + AI, never fully autonomous)
- "AI chatbot" (Moxo's agents are process-aware, not general-purpose chat)

**Connection to Agent Studio:**
Agent Studio is how HAIFLOW's AI layer is built. Reps can create custom agents trained on their own documents and policies, deployed inside workflows in five modes: Prepare (pre-fills forms), Advise (surfaces recommendations), Review (validates submissions), Automation (executes tasks), and Chat (guides participants). These are not chatbots — they are process-aware agents with context, guardrails, and a full audit trail.

## 2. What Moxo Enables — Three Pillars

### Pillar 1: Design With AI
Describe your process in plain English. Moxo's AI analyzes it and generates a complete workflow — with step types, conditional logic, parallel execution, roles, and routing — all built automatically. Operations teams go from describing a process to running it the same day. No developers. No IT. No months of implementation.

### Pillar 2: Orchestrate With AI (Agent Studio)
Create any custom AI agent, trained on your knowledge, deployed inside your workflows. These are not chatbots. They are process-aware and operate in the context of your workflows:
- **Prepare** — pre-fills form fields from prior step data before a human acts
- **Advise** — surfaces AI recommendation before the assignee acts
- **Review** — validates a submission after the human completes it; can request revisions
- **Automation** — executes tasks autonomously within the flow
- **Chat** — guides participants through completing complex form steps

Agents can be trained on your own documents and policies using RAG, connect to external tools via MCP, and choose from Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT-4o, or Google Gemini.

### Pillar 3: Run With Accountability
- **Magic Links** — no account needed for external participants; click, see full context, take action
- **AI-guided completion** — every participant gets AI assistance through their action
- **In-flow chat** — bidirectional communication between coordinators and participants
- **Full audit trail** — every action, every participant, every decision logged and traceable
- **Pulse Score (0–100)** — one number that tells you how operations are performing
- **Bottleneck detection** — AI identifies which steps are slowing processes down and recommends fixes
